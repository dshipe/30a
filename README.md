## About 30A

Scenic Highway 30A runs along Florida's South Walton coast, connecting a string of architecturally distinct beach communities including Rosemary Beach, Alys Beach, Seaside, WaterColor, Grayton Beach, and Blue Mountain Beach. Each town has its own character — together they make up one of the most unique stretches of coastline in the American South.
