Watersound: Private Community, Public Fun

Watersound on 30A is a gated community with vacation rentals and The Big Chill entertainment hub — family activities, live music, and the best Jumbotron on the Gulf.

A private gated community with vacation rentals, gulf access, and The Big Chill — 30A's liveliest family entertainment hub.

Staying at Watersound

Watersound Beach is gated but vacation rentals are available to book, giving you access to the beach and community amenities. The atmosphere is quieter and more residential than Rosemary Beach or Seaside — good for families who want space and calm.

Watersound Origins (north of Hwy 98, by St. Joe Company) can offer more affordable rentals with community pool access. A short ride to the beach.

The Big Chill

The Big Chill (formerly The Hub 30A) is the entertainment hub of the area — multiple food venues, two bars, live music stage, shopping, and a 25-foot LED Jumbotron. Parking is usually available, which is genuinely unusual on 30A.

Big Chill Food Options

  • Duos Restaurant & Bar — full service dining and drinks
  • Local Smoke — BBQ
  • Louie's Una Pizza — wood-fired pizza
  • Nectar Coffee — opens 8am
  • Mile Marker 15 Bar — cocktails, buckets of beer, game day hangout

🅿️ Parking Available

The Big Chill has its own parking lot — a rare convenience on Scenic Highway 30A. Great base for a family evening out without parking stress.
