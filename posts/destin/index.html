Destin, FL

Destin: Fishing, HarborWalk & Crab Island

HarborWalk Village, sport fishing, Big Kahuna's water park, Henderson Beach, and the legendary Crab Island sandbar.

HarborWalk Village, the world's largest sport fishing fleet, Big Kahuna's, Henderson Beach State Park, and the legendary Crab Island sandbar party.

HarborWalk Village

HarborWalk Village — jet skis, kayaks, dolphin tours, fishing charters, paddleboards. On the Destin Harbor.

HarborWalk Dining

  • Tailfins — Lively waterfront seafood. Right on the harbor.
  • Dewey Destin's — Will cook your fresh charter catch, your way, same day. A Destin classic.

Fishing

The continental shelf drops sharply just offshore, bringing deepwater game fish unusually close. Destin has the largest recreational sport fishing fleet in the US. The Destin Fishing Rodeo runs all of October — a 31-day tournament and one of the largest in the country.

Attractions

Family Activities

  • Big Kahuna's — Water park with slides, wave pools, lazy river.
  • The Track — Go-karts, mini golf, arcade.
  • Henderson Beach State Park — Quieter public beach with facilities.

Crab Island

⚓ Crab Island

Crab Island — a submerged sandbar in the Destin Harbor. Boats anchor in shallow water; floating vendors circulate; hundreds of people swim and socialise. One of the Gulf Coast's great summer scenes. Reach by water taxi from HarborWalk.
