Dune Allen Beach: Gulf Place & Great Breakfasts

Gulf Place transformed, but Perfect Pig breakfast and Skunk Gulley's Gulf views remain the best reasons to visit.

Gulf Place transformed, but Perfect Pig breakfast and Skunk Gulley's Gulf views remain as good as ever.

Gulf Place

Gulf Place has undergone significant change — once a quiet Artists' Market, now repositioned as a livelier beach-bar destination by the Alvin's Island ownership. More energy, different character. Worth visiting for the two anchors below.

The Perfect Pig

The Perfect Pig at Gulf Place is many regulars' favourite of all the Perfect Pig locations. House-smoked applewood bacon, pulled pork, careful grilled cheese, classic breakfast plates. Open breakfast and lunch. The morning queue moves steadily and is worth the wait.

Skunk Gulley

Skunk Gulley has a wide deck overlooking the Ed Walline beach access and the Gulf of Mexico — one of the best unobstructed views on Scenic Highway 30A. Casual food; the view is the draw. Late afternoon light here is exceptional.
