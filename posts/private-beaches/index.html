Things to Know

Private Beaches on 30A: What Happened & What to Do

Much of 30A's previously public beach is now restricted. Know this before you book a vacation rental.

Much of the 30A beach that was once public is now restricted. Know this before you book.

⚠️ Important for Renters

A rental listing that says "beach access" may mean only physical access to the water — not the right to set up chairs and umbrellas. Verify before you book.

What Happened

Rosemary Beach and Seaside always had private beach areas. The broader conflict began when Walton County allowed commercial chair vendors to operate in front of private homes. Homeowners (including former Governor Mike Huckabee) pushed back legally and politically. The result: previously public beaches at Seagrove, Seacrest, and Watercolor Natchez Access are now restricted.

Solutions

🌿 Guaranteed Public Beach Access

  • Grayton Beach State Park — under $10 per vehicle, in-and-out access, ample space
  • Deer Lake State Park — quieter, similar access and pricing
  • Check the Walton County Beach Access map for current public points
