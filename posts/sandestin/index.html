30A Area

Sandestin: Resorts, Outlets & Baytowne Wharf

Baytowne Wharf waterfront village, Grand Boulevard shopping, Silver Sands outlets, and the Whale's Tail beach bar.

A large resort community between Santa Rosa Beach and Destin — Baytowne Wharf, Grand Boulevard, Silver Sands outlets, and beachfront bars.

Village of Baytowne Wharf

Baytowne Wharf — waterfront village with dining, shops, bars, and nightly entertainment. One of the most active evening destinations in South Walton. Particularly good for families. On Choctawhatchee Bay.

Grand Boulevard

Grand Boulevard — upscale outdoor shopping and dining at the entrance to Sandestin. Whole Foods and Fresh Market nearby. Good for stocking the vacation rental before heading to 30A.

Silver Sands Premium Outlets

Silver Sands — one of the Southeast's largest outlet centres. 100+ designer and premium brand stores. Rainy day destination or genuine shopping trip.

Restaurants & Bars

Worth Visiting

  • Whale's Tail — Beach bar directly on the Gulf at Seascape Resort. Live webcam. Classic Gulf Coast bar experience.
  • Pompano Joe's — The original beachfront seafood institution. A Florida Panhandle classic.
