# Seacrest Beach: Hidden Gems and Free Parking

Seacrest Beach doesn't have the name recognition of its neighbours but it's one of the more practical communities on Scenic Highway 30A — and it harbours one of the corridor's great hidden-gem restaurants. The community actually spans two distinct areas: the main section sits between [Rosemary Beach](../rosemary-beach/index.html) and [Alys Beach](../alys-beach/index.html) to the east; a larger secondary area lies between Alys Beach and [WaterColor](../watercolor/index.html) to the west.

## The Best Free Parking for Rosemary Beach

The main Seacrest Beach area is where you'll find the best free public parking if you're heading to Rosemary Beach. The lot behind Peddler's Pavilion is free — a meaningful advantage on a corridor where paid parking and limited spaces are the norm. Be aware that it involves a walk of about ten minutes to reach Rosemary Beach's town centre, but for visitors who don't mind the stroll, it's far preferable to circling for a spot.

## Crabby Steve's

[Crabby Steve's](https://30a.com/locations/crabby-steves/) at 10254 E County Hwy 30A is one of those places that rewards the people who find it. It sits within a private gated community (Highpointe) and is accessible — without a pass — only from the beach side. Walk along the shoreline to reach it, and you'll find a genuinely excellent beach bar serving seafood, cold drinks, and casual plates with your feet practically in the sand. It is, as advertised, the most authentic beach bar on 30A, and the slightly inconvenient access is part of what keeps it from being overrun.

## Peddler's Pavilion

Peddler's Pavilion, just off 30A, hosts several dining and shopping options. The mix changes seasonally, so check current listings, but it's a practical stop for a casual meal if you're already parked there.

## The Western Section

The Seacrest Beach area between Alys Beach and WaterColor is quieter and can offer more affordable rental properties than the headline communities. For visitors who want reasonable prices, beach access, and the ability to cycle to [Seaside](../seaside/index.html) or [Rosemary Beach](../rosemary-beach/index.html), this section is worth considering.
