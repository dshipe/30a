# Crowds, Curfews, and When to Visit 30A
30A has always been popular, but 2020 changed things significantly. Understanding the current crowd patterns — and the communities' response to them — will help you plan a trip that matches your expectations.
## What Changed After 2020
When pandemic-related travel restrictions closed most of the country in spring 2020, Florida was among the first states to reopen. By Memorial Day 2020, 30A was accepting visitors again when most other beach destinations were still closed, and the result was an enormous surge in tourism that has never fully retreated. Simultaneously, low mortgage interest rates and the shift to remote work drove a housing boom along 30A — new construction increased the supply of rental properties but also changed the character of some quieter communities. The combination of post-pandemic pent-up demand, new residents, and new vacation rentals created a significantly busier, more expensive 30A than existed before 2020.
## Spring Break
The spring break situation became genuinely problematic in 2021 and 2022. 30A businesses close at approximately 10pm — often beginning to wind down at 9:30pm. It is not a nightlife destination and was never designed to be one. Large groups of college-age spring breakers found themselves in an area with nowhere to go after 9pm, resulting in well-documented scenes of crowds milling aimlessly around a closed Seaside late at night. Local law enforcement had limited tools to manage outdoor gatherings that weren't technically illegal. In response, several communities introduced spring break curfews for minors, and the practice of explicitly not marketing to the spring break demographic intensified across 30A businesses and rental companies.
## When to Visit
The best times to visit 30A are early May and late September to mid-October. The weather remains warm (water temperatures in the low-to-mid 80s in early May; upper 70s in late September), the crowds are noticeably smaller, restaurants have more availability, and rental prices can drop significantly. If you must travel in peak summer season, June tends to be less crowded than July and August, when school holidays fill every available property.
