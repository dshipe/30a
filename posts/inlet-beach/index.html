Inlet Beach

Right next to Rosemary Beach with more affordable rentals, a beach tunnel, and great evening dining.

Right next to Rosemary Beach but with more affordable rentals — Inlet Beach is 30A's smartest base camp.

Getting to the Beach

Many Inlet Beach rentals sit on the north side of US Highway 98 — a meaningful cost saving over Gulf-front Rosemary Beach prices. A pedestrian and cycling tunnel runs under the highway, connecting north-side properties directly to the beach side. Families with kids and bikes will appreciate not having to cross a busy four-lane road. Walk through and you're at the Gulf in under a minute.

Dining

Cuvée 30A is the headliner — led by FRLA Restaurateur of the Year Chef Tim Creehan, this award-winning restaurant combines casual fine dining with an extensive wine list. It's genuinely one of the better restaurants on all of Scenic Highway 30A and worth a reservation for a proper evening out.

The Donut Hole (12983 Hwy 98 East) opens at 6am and closes at 10pm. The key lime pie is the famous item, but the breakfast draws the Sunday morning lines. Expect a wait in season — it moves, and it's worth it.

Kids' Playground

Families will appreciate the well-equipped playground at 19 Windward Lane — a popular neighborhood play area for a break from the beach.

📍 Plan Your Visit

Stay here, save money, then cycle west into Rosemary Beach for dinner or east toward Alys Beach for a morning walk. You get all the access at a fraction of the cost.
