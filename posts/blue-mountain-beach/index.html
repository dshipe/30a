Blue Mountain Beach: Quiet and Underrated

One of 30A's quieter communities — great for cycling through, with Red Fish Taco and Blue Mountain Creamery as highlights.

Named for blue lupine wildflowers, one of 30A's highest points, and home to two reliably great casual food stops.

Red Fish Taco

Red Fish Taco (2052 W County Hwy 30A) is one of the most enjoyable casual meals on 30A Florida — fresh tacos, relaxed atmosphere, usually no long wait even in season. Live music events seasonally. Check the calendar at redfishtaco.com before you visit.

Blue Mountain Creamery

Blue Mountain Creamery is a long-time local favourite for handcrafted ice cream with rotating seasonal flavours. Standard stop for families on the Timpoochee Trail.

📍 A Good Through-Stop

Less crowded beach access, scenic high dunes, and two reliable food stops make Blue Mountain Beach a perfect mid-ride break on a longer 30A cycling day.
