# Seagrove Beach: The Best Value on 30A

If I'm staying on 30A, Seagrove Beach is where I want to be. It sits immediately east of [Seaside](../seaside/index.html) — close enough to walk, cycle, or golf-cart across in under ten minutes — but with rental prices that are noticeably more reasonable than the heart of Seaside itself. The community is quiet and unpretentious, the beach access is among the best on the entire corridor, and the restaurant options are excellent.

## Beach Access

Seagrove Beach has some of the largest and most accessible public beach access points on Scenic Highway 30A. The main access from Highway 395 and the points east along 30A give you ample space to set up for the day without the density you'll encounter in Seaside or Rosemary Beach. This is one of the reasons it's a favourite among families with young children and visitors who want room to breathe on the sand.

## Dining

[Surfing Deer](https://www.yelp.com/biz/surfing-deer-seagrove-beach) (2743 E County Hwy 30A) is the local dinner spot — a warmly-lit, casual restaurant that's become something of an institution in Seagrove. Open for dinner from 4:30pm, it gets busy but the wait is manageable. [Café 30A](https://maps.google.com/?q=Cafe+30A+Seagrove+Beach+FL) is consistently rated among the most reliable dining experiences on the entire highway — the kind of place where everything is done right, the menu is focused, and you leave satisfied every time.

[The Perfect Pig](https://www.theperfectpig.com/location/seagrove-beach/) at 4281 E County Hwy 30A is the original and, to many regular visitors, the best of the several Perfect Pig locations. Open for breakfast and lunch only (8am-3pm), it draws queues on weekend mornings. The pulled pork breakfast is the signature; the house-smoked meats are exceptional.

## The Lodge at Greenway Station

[Greenway Station](https://greenway30a.com/) is the small commercial hub at the heart of Seagrove Beach, and The Lodge here is one of the very few hotels on 30A. It offers a comfortable, well-positioned alternative to vacation rentals for those who prefer hotel-style accommodation without committing to the more expensive Pearl or WaterColor Inn.
