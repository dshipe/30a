Seagrove Beach: The Best Value on 30A

Seagrove Beach is a local favourite on 30A — close to Seaside by bike, more affordable rentals, the largest public beach access, and some of the best breakfast on the highway.

A personal favourite on 30A — close to Seaside by bike, more affordable rentals, the largest public beach access, and three restaurants worth seeking out.

Why Stay Here

Seagrove sits immediately east of Seaside — a 10-minute cycle away — with noticeably more affordable rental prices. The community is quiet, the beach access is among the best on 30A Florida, and the restaurants are excellent. For value-conscious visitors, this is the sweet spot.

Beach Access

Seagrove Beach has the largest public beach access points on Scenic Highway 30A — from Highway 395 and east along 30A. More space, less density than Seaside or Rosemary Beach. Families with children will particularly appreciate the room to breathe.

Restaurants

Where to Eat

  • Surfing Deer — 2743 E County Hwy 30A. Dinner from 4:30pm. Casual, warmly-lit, local favourite.
  • Café 30A — Consistently rated among the best dining experiences on the entire 30A corridor.
  • The Perfect Pig — 4281 E County Hwy 30A. Breakfast & lunch, 8am–3pm. The original location; house-smoked meats, pulled pork breakfast.

The Lodge at Greenway Station

Greenway Station is Seagrove's small commercial hub. The Lodge here is one of the very few hotels on 30A — a comfortable option for those who prefer hotel-style accommodation over vacation rentals.
